About

Bio but actually it's whatever came out of indecision

I'm Daniel Firebanks-Quevedo, a computer scientist and musician. I grew up in the vibrant city of Lima (Peru), and have been unexpectedly fortunate to live next to a castle in Montezuma (New Mexico) [insert link to uwc and a pic], right by downtown Budapest (Hungary) [pic1], and overlooking the Hudson River in New York City [pic2].

After some time living in the midst of middles of nowhere, I'm back in the city life, working as a machine learning engineer for a small startup called IBM (lmao), in their Watson Natural Language Understanding team. I also try to be involved in data science for social good projects, most of which have led me to work with extremely inspiring and kind fellows, and sometimes end up in publications [link lol].

If I didn't spend my day screaming at computers, I would be either a cook, street magician, musician, dancer, bartender, professor, or surgeon. Some of those things I enjoy doing in my free time. Others are just delusions.

I'm not generally an indecisive person, but when I am, it is about what to write in my bio. So I guess that's a fun fact about me - will update this section later! [and add a personal pic]
